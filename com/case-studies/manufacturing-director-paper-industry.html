Manufacturing Director — Paper Industry, Asia

Manufacturing sector executive search delivering specialist production leadership for a new high-specification facility.

Manufacturing

The Challenge

Following an enquiry from a large conglomerate, MSC Headhunting was retained as manufacturing headhunters to find a new factory manager for the paper industry. After detailed consultation with the company owners, we gained a thorough understanding of the business and role. The company had an extremely urgent requirement: they had recently purchased a site and built a brand new high-specification production line. They needed an expert to ensure a smooth start-up, maintain steady production rates, and optimise the equipment to realise its full theoretical capacity.

The equipment was brand new and imported from abroad, meaning only a small number of specialists worldwide had the knowledge to run and maintain this specific technology. Every week without production represented significant financial losses, creating intense time pressure.

Our Approach

Our teams worked around the clock to deliver the shortlist within three weeks. The international scope of the assignment required conducting face-to-face interviews across multiple countries including the United States, Canada, Germany, Belgium, United Kingdom, Austria, and Romania. We coordinated closely with local teams to ensure comprehensive coverage of the limited global talent pool for this highly specialised role.

The Result

A shortlist of four of the world's leading specialists was presented to the client, all of whom were willing to relocate. The candidates were invited for meetings at the factory and company headquarters. The most experienced individual was hired quickly, made immediate progress, and enabled the site to begin production within weeks. The company has since become a leading manufacturer, selling domestically and exporting to international markets.
