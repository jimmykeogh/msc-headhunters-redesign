Finance Director — United Kingdom

Board-level financial leadership for a major UK technology firm.

Finance

The Challenge

A UK technology firm with £80 million annual turnover lost their Finance Director to a competitor. The departing director agreed to a 3-month notice period to help find his successor, creating a firm deadline for the search.

MSC Headhunting was selected based on their three guarantees and track record in finding finance executives quickly. A detailed briefing occurred with the CEO, outgoing Finance Director, and MSC Project Director to discuss role requirements, company culture, structure, products, services, and future plans.

Our Approach

The research team mapped the competitive landscape and identified candidates while respecting client-specified strategic alliances that could not be approached. Over 100 prospective candidates were contacted across the UK finance and technology sectors.

Confidentiality presented a particular challenge — many candidates knew key people at the firm. MSC introduced mutual NDAs to replace standard one-way agreements, protecting both parties throughout the process.

Twenty top candidates were interviewed by phone and video. The best underwent half-day face-to-face meetings with the MSC Project Director. Five finalists received detailed reports covering experience, background, character, suitability, and motivation.

The Result

Shortlisted candidates met the client at neutral venues over several days. Two preferred candidates met the CEO and Chairman. Following unanimous agreement, the selected candidate was offered the role.

After negotiating a shorter notice period, both Finance Directors worked together briefly for a smooth transition, ensuring continuity of financial leadership for the business.
