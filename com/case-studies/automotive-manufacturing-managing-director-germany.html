Geschäftsführer (Managing Director) — Automotive Manufacturing, Germany

Automotive sector executive search enabling European market entry with full leadership and sales team placement.

Automotive

The Challenge

A large Far Eastern manufacturer of specialist automotive parts was ready to establish roots in the European market. Having previously sold on a small scale from their headquarters, they had decided to make the necessary investment to develop the business properly. After detailed consultation, they recognised the need for manufacturing industry headhunters to set up a legal entity in Germany and hire a senior local executive with market experience. MSC Headhunting was retained to search for a Geschäftsführer (Managing Director) followed by a sales manager and a team of Key Account Managers.

As the client manufactured highly specialist automotive parts, it was imperative that the candidates MSC approached had specific technical knowledge and understanding. All parties required mutual confidentiality due to their high-profile positions and the client's need to keep their European expansion plans hidden from competitors.

Our Approach

The initial research phase was crucial in identifying the relevant companies from which to source candidates. Over 80 industry executives were approached within the German market, and the ten strongest individuals were interviewed locally and confidentially. The search required balancing deep automotive sector expertise with the entrepreneurial drive needed to build a business from the ground up in a new market.

The Result

After only four weeks, MSC presented a shortlist of the most suitable, qualified, and motivated candidates with relevant networks to ensure swift market traction. The preferred candidate visited the company headquarters, toured the factories, and was presented with a contract. Careful negotiations reduced the notice period, enabling MSC to begin the second phase: working with the new Managing Director to hire a sales manager and a team of eight Key Account Managers across the D-A-CH region, Nordics, Netherlands, France, Eastern Europe, and the United Kingdom.

Within four months, MSC had enabled this client to hire an MD and a full sales team, establishing a sales pipeline within the first year of European operations. The company now holds prestigious client references across Europe and is generating revenue after less than a year.

“MSC Headhunting provided us with everything they promised and within a very fast time frame. Now that we have a sales team the company already has some prestigious client references here in Europe and so is generating revenue after less than a year.”
Client Far Eastern Automotive Manufacturer
