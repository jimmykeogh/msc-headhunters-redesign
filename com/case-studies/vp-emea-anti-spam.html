Vice President EMEA Sales — Email Security, Europe

Urgent cybersecurity leadership placement delivered within two weeks.

Technology

The Challenge

MSC Headhunting was urgently contacted by the VP of HR at a world-leading email security company requiring a Vice President of Sales for the EMEA region. The client needed a shortlist within 2 weeks — described as their most challenging assignment yet.

The urgency was driven by the company’s planned IPO announcement, which required a full management team to be in place. There was no room for delay.

Our Approach

The team contacted over 70 prospective candidates from IT security markets across EMEA, including executives from anti-virus, anti-spam, encryption, defence, cyber-security, firewall and security testing companies.

The project demanded exceptional rigour and pace:

  • Bi-weekly client updates rather than the standard weekly cadence
  • Special NDAs prepared by the client’s lawyers for all candidate interactions
  • Working across multiple time zones and weekends
  • Strict confidentiality protocols throughout

The Result

The team delivered an exceptional shortlist of world-class IT security sales VPs within the guaranteed 2-week timeframe. This enabled the client to announce their IPO plans with the full management team in place — a critical milestone for the business.

“MSC Headhunting delivered a superior service never experienced through our traditional recruitment methods. Retaining MSC Headhunters was crucial for the company’s overall plan, perfect timing and partnership.”
VP of HR World-Leading Email Security Company
