CTO — eCommerce Platform Provider, France

Technology leadership placement delivered ahead of schedule for a pre-IPO platform.

eCommerce

The Challenge

An international eCommerce platform provider based in Paris needed a new Chief Technology Officer after their previous CTO left for a competitor. The company was approaching a critical phase — preparing for an IPO — and required strong technology leadership to be in place quickly.

The client originally considered using a recruitment agency but recognised that the sensitivity and confidential urgency of the role demanded a retained headhunting approach. They selected MSC based on the firm’s three unique headhunting guarantees.

Our Approach

The retained search was comprehensive and rigorous:

  • Over 100 candidates approached across the European eCommerce sector
  • The best 20 candidates screened, then narrowed to 10 for in-person interviews
  • A final shortlist of 4 world-class candidates presented with detailed reports
  • The entire process completed in 6 weeks — 2 weeks ahead of the guaranteed timeline

The Result

The client hired two candidates from the shortlist — one as CTO and one as VP of Engineering — significantly strengthening their technology leadership ahead of the planned IPO. The dual appointment demonstrated both the depth and quality of MSC’s candidate pipeline.

“We originally considered using a recruitment agency to fill our vacancy, however because of the sensitivity and confidential urgency, we decided to engage a reputable Headhunting firm. MSC Headhunters was by far the most professional and experienced to manage our vacancy. Within weeks we had a shortlist of candidates, weeks later we hired two of them.”
