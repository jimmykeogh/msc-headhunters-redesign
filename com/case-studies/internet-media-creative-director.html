Creative Director — Internet Media, United Kingdom

Creative leadership blending artistic vision with digital content strategy.

Media

The Challenge

MSC Headhunting was retained to find a Creative Director for an emerging Internet Media company in the UK. The company offered web design, SEO, newsletters, blogs, articles, short films, animation, and online magazines across Europe.

The company needed an experienced Creative Director to support expansion in a competitive market. The role demanded a rare blend of artistic vision, commercial awareness, and digital content strategy expertise.

Our Approach

Following detailed discussions about requirements, culture, and organisational structure, the MSC team identified suitable candidates and began outreach across the digital media landscape.

Candidates showed positive interest due to the creative freedom on offer, involvement in commercial strategy, and personal growth opportunities. The company prioritised client relationships and a positive work environment — values that resonated strongly with the strongest candidates.

The Result

A successful candidate was hired within four weeks and began immediately after probation. Within 12 weeks, he had earned respect from staff and clients alike while bringing valuable contacts and network credibility to the organisation.

“The service I received from MSC was extremely impressive. They have a passion for finding the right person for the position which is second to none. The urgency, professional approach and friendly manner I experienced ensured the process was enjoyable, hassle free and saved time.”
Client Internet Media Company, UK
