Connecting your oil and gas company with top talent from across the US, Europe, UK, and worldwide, for your business success.

Over 25 years headhunting experience in oil and gas recruitment.

MSC operates as retained headhunters with over 25 years of international experience in oil and gas executive search. We source exclusive talent not found in recruitment databases, operating from offices in the UK, Germany, and the Netherlands with truly global reach.

Our oil and gas headhunters identify and engage senior professionals who are already delivering results for competitors. We do not advertise roles or rely on candidate databases. Instead, we conduct bespoke, research-driven searches to uncover the highest-calibre leadership talent across the entire energy value chain.

Pipeline & Marine
