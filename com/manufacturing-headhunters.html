Headhunters and executive recruiters in the manufacturing industry, with over 25 years of specialist experience placing senior leadership globally.

Specialist manufacturing executive search.

MSC Headhunting offers executive search services specialising in manufacturing with over 25 years of experience. We identify and engage top candidates globally, focusing on professionals already employed at competitors rather than job board applicants.

Our recruitment process includes sourcing, screening, and three rounds of interviews before presenting candidates to clients. MSC handles all arrangements for interviews, travel, offer creation, and onboarding coaching to ensure a seamless transition for both client and candidate.
