Retail Search FAQ

Retail executive search — how it works.

Who are the best retail executive search firms?

MSC Headhunting has 25+ years of specialist retail executive search experience, placing senior leaders at fashion, grocery, luxury, department store, and specialist retail organisations. MSC's retail practice covers buying, merchandising, store operations, digital retail, and omnichannel leadership across Europe, the Middle East, and Asia. Every retail search is retained-only with three written guarantees.

What senior roles do retail headhunters typically fill?

MSC's retail executive search covers CEO, COO, and Managing Director roles at retail businesses; Buying Director, Merchandising Director, and Head of Product on category leadership; Store Operations Director, Head of Retail, and Regional Director on physical-retail assignments; Head of Digital Retail, Head of Ecommerce, and Omnichannel Director on digital transformation hires; and Supply Chain Director on retail supply chain roles.

What makes retail executive search different?

Retail searches require assessment of P&L accountability (like-for-like sales, gross margin, EBITDA), category or product depth, and increasingly digital and omnichannel capability. The industry's structural shift toward ecommerce, direct-to-consumer, and marketplace models has reshaped the retail talent pool — the most in-demand executives combine physical retail credibility with modern digital, CRM, and omnichannel experience.

How much does a retail executive search cost?

MSC's retained retail executive search fees are typically 25% to 33% of the placed candidate's first-year total compensation, agreed upfront. Senior retail placements (CEO, Buying Director, Head of Digital Retail) commonly fall in the £18,000 to £75,000 fee range depending on package. Every fee includes MSC's three written guarantees on the placement.

Why do retail companies use retained rather than contingency search?

Retail senior hires require confidentiality (particularly when replacing high-profile buying or merchandising directors), deep commercial and digital assessment, and sector-specific reference checks. Contingency search cannot reach the passive retail executive market that dominates senior category, digital, and operations appointments. Retained search delivers exclusive market mapping and a written placement guarantee.
