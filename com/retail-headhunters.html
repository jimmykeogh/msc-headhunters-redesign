Retail headhunting services.

MSC Headhunting works extensively within the retail sector, identifying and attracting experienced candidates to fill senior vacancies. The UK retail industry is inherently dynamic because of economic and political affairs affecting consumer and business confidence. The retail industry continues to be a vital part of the economy, with millions employed in the sector.

We serve diverse retail businesses including retail chains, luxury boutiques, fashion, grocery, logistics, eCommerce, and related sectors. Our retail headhunters understand the unique pressures of the sector and identify leaders who can drive commercial performance in an increasingly omnichannel environment.

Typical retail roles we source.

  • eCommerce Director
  • Senior Retail Director
  • Supply Chain Director
  • Head of eCommerce
  • Account Director
  • Head of Retail
  • Business Development Director
  • Global Marketing Manager
  • Operations Manager
  • Digital Marketing Manager
  • Lead Developer

An emerging market demands exceptional leadership.

The retail landscape is undergoing fundamental transformation as consumers increasingly expect seamless omnichannel experiences. From luxury boutiques to major retail chains, the demand for senior leaders who understand both physical and digital retail has never been greater.

MSC Headhunting’s retail headhunters identify commercially minded executives who can navigate this complexity, drive growth, and build the agile teams needed to succeed in modern retail. We operate on a retained basis only, with no candidate databases, ensuring every search delivers genuinely exceptional talent.
