Our Story

Founded in 1998, MSC Headhunting is a retained executive search firm.

With offices in the UK, Germany, and the Netherlands, MSC has spent over two decades building deep relationships across industries and borders. We specialise in identifying, approaching, and placing C-level executives, board members, and senior leaders for organisations that demand the best.

Our team of bilingual consultants brings sector-specific expertise, cultural intelligence, and an unwavering commitment to finding people who transform organisations. We operate 24/7, reflecting the global nature of today’s talent markets.

MSC holds preferred supplier status with leading venture capital and private equity firms, and has acted as advisers to the European Parliament on matters of international talent mobility.

We believe the right leader at the right time can fundamentally reshape an organisation’s trajectory. That belief drives every search we undertake.
