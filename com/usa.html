Over 25 years serving U.S. corporations worldwide.

MSC Headhunters operates globally, servicing U.S. clients by finding specialist expert candidates from around the world and enabling relocation to the United States or establishing satellite offices in international markets. Our service is discreet and confidential throughout.

The firm has over 25 years of experience working with U.S. corporations and sources executives across multiple industries. We identify and attract the best candidates from competitors, successfully relocate international senior staff, and use unique search methods with extensive networks to find anyone from anywhere.

Our Approach

Dedicated in-house research teams delivering results.

01

Identify & Attract

We identify and attract the best candidates from competitors who wouldn’t otherwise engage with job postings, using unique search methods and extensive networks.

02

International Relocation

We successfully relocate international senior staff to the United States and help establish satellite offices in new international markets.

03

In-House Research

Our dedicated in-house research teams actively identify approximately 100 candidates per role, narrowing to 3–5 finalists through rigorous screening.

04

Director-Led Interviews

MSC Directors conduct detailed technical and personal interviews before delivering a final shortlist of fully vetted candidates.
