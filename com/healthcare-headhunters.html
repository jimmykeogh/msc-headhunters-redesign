Healthcare executive search.

Total healthcare expenditure in the UK accounts for around 10% of gross domestic product (GDP), making it one of the most significant sectors in the economy. The demand for exceptional leadership talent across healthcare organisations has never been greater.

MSC Headhunting provides retained executive search services across the full healthcare value chain, from pharmaceutical companies and biotech firms to medical device manufacturers and healthcare service providers. Our healthcare headhunters combine deep sector knowledge with a rigorous, confidential search methodology.
