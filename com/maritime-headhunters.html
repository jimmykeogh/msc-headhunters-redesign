Shipping & maritime headhunters, including ports & logistics.

Connecting industry leaders from across the globe, MSC Headhunters specialises in finding executive talent for maritime businesses, including shipping, ports, and logistics.

Experts in shipping headhunter & maritime executive search.

MSC operates globally with over 25 years of maritime headhunting experience. Our offices located in the UK, Germany, and the Netherlands provide international reach with local expertise, enabling us to connect the world's leading maritime organisations with exceptional leadership talent.

As retained headhunters, we do not rely on candidate databases. Instead, we conduct bespoke, research-driven searches to identify and engage the highest-calibre executives who are already delivering results within the maritime and shipping sectors. Our approach ensures you access talent that traditional recruitment methods simply cannot reach.

Over 25 years of maritime & shipping headhunter industry excellence.

MSC Headhunting has built a proven track record placing senior leaders across global fleets, port authorities, and marine engineering organisations. We operate on a retained search basis only, offering three guarantees on all assignments to ensure long-term success for our clients.
