We headhunt high quality candidates across the media industry.

MSC has over 25 years experience in entertainment and media headhunting, securing broadcasting, publishing, digital media, and creative executive talent worldwide.

Media & entertainment executive search.

The media and entertainment sector is worth over £74 billion annually and employs around 6% of the workforce. In this dynamic and fast-moving industry, the quality of senior leadership directly determines competitive success.

MSC Headhunting provides a full-circle recruitment solution for media organisations, operating on a retained fee basis only. We source talent confidentially from competitors, conducting bespoke, tailored projects without reliance on candidate databases. Our media headhunters understand the unique creative and commercial pressures of the industry and identify leaders who can deliver in both dimensions.
