Global Reach

European headhunters operating from three continental bases.

Operating from the United Kingdom, Germany, and the Netherlands, our multi-cultural, bilingual consulting team brings first-hand knowledge of local business practices, regulatory environments, and cultural nuances critical to successful international placements. As established German headhunters and Netherlands headhunters, we understand the commercial landscapes of continental Europe at a granular level.

Our focus is on passive candidates already excelling in their current roles. By combining deep market knowledge with emphasis on cultural compatibility, we ensure every candidate is technically outstanding and the right fit for your organisation’s unique environment.
